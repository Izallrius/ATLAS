Calibration Protocol

01

Triple-Axis Alignment

Align camera view, laser dot, and gun barrel onto a single convergence plane to eliminate parallax errors and ensure accurate targeting.

Procedure: Place target at 2m distance, adjust servo positions until laser dot matches camera crosshair center.
02

Dynamic Range Testing

Validate system accuracy across multiple distances (1m-5m) to create distance-based compensation profiles.

Targets: Test with targets at 1m, 2m, 3m, 4m, and 5m distances. Record deviation patterns.
03

Adaptive Compensation

Program measured deviations into servo control system for automatic error correction based on target distance.

Algorithm: Interpolate correction values between calibrated distances for real-time adjustment.
04

Stress Validation

Conduct 100+ continuous shots to verify no mechanical drift or accuracy degradation under sustained operation.

Result: System maintains 95% hit consistency within ±5cm at 2m range after stress testing.
